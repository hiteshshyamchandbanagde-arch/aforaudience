Democratise live performance art by giving artists a stage, organisers a command center, venues a smart OS, and audiences the most immersive way to discover and experience live art.

## 3. USER ROLES & PERSONAS

### Role 1: Artist (Comedian / Poet / Musician)
**Who:** Creative performers seeking gigs, exposure, and fans
**Goals:** Get booked, grow fanbase, track career growth
**Pain points:** No centralised platform, manual networking, no analytics

### Role 2: Organiser
**Who:** Event managers, comedy clubs, poetry collectives
**Goals:** Post events, fill seats, manage lineups, find venues
**Pain points:** Scattered tools, manual ticketing, no artist discovery

### Role 3: Venue Owner
**Who:** Cafe owners, theater managers, rooftop space owners
**Goals:** Monetise space, fill calendar, attract quality organisers
**Pain points:** No dedicated platform, manual availability tracking

### Role 4: Audience
**Who:** Art lovers, comedy fans, poetry enthusiasts
**Goals:** Discover shows, book tickets, review experiences
**Pain points:** No single platform for all live art in India

### Role 5: Admin
**Who:** AforAudience platform team
**Goals:** Approve registrations, manage quality, grow ecosystem
**Pain points:** Manual approval processes, fraud detection

## 12. PAYMENT FLOW

### Audience Booking Flow
1. Select event → Choose seats
2. Review order (seats + amount)
3. Click "Book Now" → Redirect to Razorpay
4. Pay via UPI / Card / Wallet / Net Banking
5. Payment verified via webhook
6. Booking confirmed in DB
7. PDF ticket + SMS + Email sent instantly

### Organiser Payout Flow
1. Tickets sold → Platform holds funds
2. Event completed → T+2 settlement
3. Platform deducts commission (5-10%)
4. Auto payout to organiser bank account
5. Dashboard shows real-time earnings

### Artist Tip Flow
1. Audience loads tip wallet (UPI/Card)
2. Clicks "Tip Artist" after show
3. Selects amount (₹50 / ₹100 / ₹200 / custom)
4. 100% goes directly to artist wallet
5. Artist receives instant notification
